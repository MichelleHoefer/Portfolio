A little about me

Hi, I'm Michelle.

Hi, I'm Michelle - an aspiring software developer with a passion for building smart, scalable solutions that make a real impact. After 15 years in the dental industry, I transitioned into tech, bringing with me a sharp eye for detail, strong communication skills, and a deep appreciation for problem-solving.

I am currently sharpening my skills through SheCodes Bootcamp, where I have gained hands-on experience in Python, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, APIs, React and version control with GitHub. I enjoy working behind the scenes to design efficient systems and love digging into logic and data.

Whether it's building back-end architecture or collaborating across teams, I am excited to contribute to meaningful projects that challenge me to grow. Let's build something great together.

Away from the computer I enjoy spending time with my greyhound, Deacon, learning Korean and indulging in a bit of amateur art.
