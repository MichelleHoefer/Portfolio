A little about me...

Hi, I'm Michelle – a motivated career changer with a growing passion for software development. After 15 years in the dental industry, I'm now pursuing a more fulfilling path where I can combine creativity, logic, and problem-solving.

I’ve completed the SheCodes Bootcamp, gaining hands-on experience with Python, React, APIs, responsive web design, and Git version control. I particularly enjoy working behind the scenes to design efficient systems and explore the logic behind how things work. My learning journey is ongoing: I’m currently completing the “100 Days of Code: The Complete Python Bootcamp” by Angela Yu and plan to undertake a data science course next. I’m open to diverse opportunities – both within and beyond traditional tech – where I can bring my drive, adaptability, and fresh perspective to a team or project that values curiosity and growth.

Away from the computer I enjoy spending time with my greyhound, Deacon, learning Korean, going to music events and indulging in a bit of amateur art.
